Software Engineer

Are you interested in creating features and solving problems across a wide variety of products ranging from memberships and trainings to livestream and video-on-demand platforms?

Join us and help us solve difficult problems. You have the opportunity to ship code that will help millions of users connect with instructors and meet their personal fitness goals.

You can make our world happier and healthier.

What you can expect

  • Own a feature from conception to deployment.

  • Work with a supportive team in a collaborative environment.

  • Grow your skills with new projects that will challenge you.

  • Attend conferences to keep apprised of new things in the tech industry.

What skills do you need

  1. Solid understanding of PHP 8.

  2. Knowledge of creating schema and querying MySQL (Aurora).

  3. Javascript with experience working with modern libraries such as Vue 3 or React.

  4. Experience with or knowledge of working with service-oriented architecture (SOA).

  5. Ability to communicate clearly with team mates and non-technical staff.

What would make you successful in this position

  • Knows typescript or has a willingness to learn it.

  • Has the ability to write unit tests and an understanding as to why it is important.

  • Has a habit of writing clear documentation on how a process works.

UI/UX Designer

As the largest branded fitness program in the world, Zumba™ brings millions of people together every day.

About the team

You'll be working with a small but might group of collaborative designers on responsive websites (Zumba.com, StrongNation.com, etc), Instructor iOS and Android Apps (ClassBuzz and Zin Play), and the new Sommos.fit creator and fitness management platform. The team is a part of the Zumba Technology department, where you'll partner with Product Management and Engineering to better Zumba's product experiences.

About you

You've got experience with product KPIs, comfortable with research/data, understand design-thinking, and passionate about pixels and innovation. You enjoy digging-in to truly understand your customers and their behavior, then creating amazing experiences to help solve their problems. And, as a bonus, you've got a killer sense of humor.

What you'll do

  • Execute end-to-end design efforts across all our platforms, from conception with stakeholders to final implementation with engineering.

  • Take on full ownership of a variety of high-impact initiatives & product experiences.

  • Work alongside our Product Managers and Engineers to improve product experiences, uncover new opportunities, define their KPIs and prioritize them accordingly.

  • Conduct design sprints, workshops, ongoing and project based research, usability studies, and data analysis.

  • Create lo-fi sketches, wireframes, and fully designed mockups and prototypes that are based on and add to our design system.

  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams on the overall design strategy for new products.

  • Present research, UX strategy, end to end experiences, interaction models and screen designs to stakeholders and cross-functional teams.

Deliverables: UX Strategy decks, User research presentations, Usability testing plans, Wireframes, Mock ups, and Prototypes

Basic tools: Sketch, Figma, Invision, Jira, and Slack

Your background

  • 3-5 years of relevant experience in design of digital products that have been released to market, with a proven track record of success.

  • Minimum 2+ years of mobile apps experience.

  • Strong portfolio displaying user-centered design and interaction design best practices.

  • Strong design and typography skills.

  • Attention to detail, organized, thorough and precise with work.

  • Collaborative and receptive to feedback.

  • Ability to integrate into engineering and QA processes to ensure high quality execution.

  • A solid grasp of user-centered design (UCD), planning and conducting user research, A/B testing, heuristic analysis, usability and accessibility requirements.

  • Ability to clearly and effectively communicate design processes, ideas, and solutions to teams.

  • Comfortable presenting to large audiences using Google Slides or similar.

  • Basic HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript skills are a plus.
