Software Engineer

Are you interested in creating features and solving problems across a wide variety of products ranging from memberships and trainings to livestream and video-on-demand platforms?

Join us and help us solve difficult problems. You have the opportunity to ship code that will help millions of users connect with instructors and meet their personal fitness goals.

You can make our world happier and healthier.

What you can expect

  • Own a feature from conception to deployment.

  • Work with a supportive team in a collaborative environment.

  • Grow your skills with new projects that will challenge you.

  • Attend conferences to keep apprised of new things in the tech industry.

What skills do you need

  1. Solid understanding of PHP 8.

  2. Knowledge of creating schema and querying MySQL (Aurora).

  3. Javascript with experience working with modern libraries such as Vue 3 or React.

  4. Experience with or knowledge of working with service-oriented architecture (SOA).

  5. Ability to communicate clearly with team mates and non-technical staff.

What would make you successful in this position

  • Knows typescript or has a willingness to learn it.

  • Has the ability to write unit tests and an understanding as to why it is important.

  • Has a habit of writing clear documentation on how a process works.
